📖 How to Order Medicines

🌐 Online Method (For Users)

Step 1:

Create an account or login to your existing account.

Step 2:

Go to "View Medicines" and browse available stock with prices.

Step 3:

Select the medicine you need, enter quantity, phone number, and delivery address.

Step 4:

Click "Place Order" – done! Track status in your dashboard.

Step 5:

Receive notifications when your order is processed and bill is ready.
